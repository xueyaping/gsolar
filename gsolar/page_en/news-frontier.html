New Technology

Gsolar Power Co.,Ltd has been developing solar cell and solar module testing technology and related testing equipment for many years, which is a high-tech enterprise integrating R&D, design, production, sales and services. We have one R&D center and two production bases. There are three series of products, including more than 20 types, such as solar simulator for solar cell and solar module, outdoor module array tester, EL/PL detector of solar cell and module, PL detector of wafer, automatic solar module assembly line and ect. We serve more than 500 global companies and export equipment to Germany, Italy, Spain, America, Japan, India and so on.

Gsolar has a strong R&D team, including Director of Chinese Renewable Energy, experts selected for New Century Talents Project, experts enjoying the State Council Special Allowance, researchers, university professors, senior engineers and so on. The members of R&D are more than one third of the total members. The core engineers have more than 20-year experience in PV industry, finishing quite a number of national key projects.
