Company Profile

Established in 2006, Gsolar Power Co.,Ltd is a professional manufacturer specialized in the research,development and production of advanced technological, beautiful appearance and top quality PV testing equipment. We started researching PV technologies from 1990, having a history of nearly 25 years. We have one R&D center and two production bases and have over 150 employees, among whom, more than 50 are R&D engineers.

We developed and produced the first EL and PL detector in China. Our products range from solar wafer testing equipment to the silicon solar module assembly line, mainly including solar wafer photoluminescence detector, solar cell tester, solar cell electroluminescence detector, solar module electroluminescence detector, IEC AAA solar simulator, super AAA solar simulator, IV&EL detector, solar module assembly line, and etc, totally more than 30 kinds of products.

All of our products comply with the international quality standards, even beyond the international standards. Gsolar is the only PV equipment supplier that has both TUV and CE certificates in China. We have passed ISO9000, TUV certificate, CE certificate, ITRI certificate (industrial Technology Research Institute), more than 20 patent certificates. Besides, we develop and propose 2-3 new products to the market every year, the certificate increases at a speed of 6 copies per year averagely.

As a result of high quality products and outstanding customer service, we have gained a global sales network. We own the top one market share in China, and export equipment to more than 30 countries, such as Germany, France, USA, England, Italy, Spain, Romania, Poland, Croatia, Turkey, Japan, India, Malaysia, Africa, and so on. Quite a number of globally famous solar cell and module producers, such as YingLi Solar, Hanergy, Sun-Earth, HareonSolar, Trina, Hanwha SolarOne, JinKo Solar, Renesola, Solar semiconductor, Flectronics all are using our equipment. Besides, some famous solar module assembly line suppliers, such as Schmid in Germany, Mondragon in Spain use our equipment in their turn key line project.
